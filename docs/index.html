**English Poem:**

Tomorrow's forecast, a tale to tell,
Of three locations, with weather to compel.
Punta Arenas, Chile, with clouds so gray,
Max temp of 7.1, a chilly day.
Precipitation high, 9.4, a rainy sight,
Max wind of 46.1, a gusty, blustery night.
Aalborg, Denmark, with clouds so bright,
Max temp of 18.8, a warm and sunny light.
But Viña del Mar, with a different tale,
No precipitation, a clear and sunny gale.
Max wind of 5.7, a gentle, soothing breeze,
A perfect day, with warm and sunny ease.

The differences are stark, the contrasts so grand,
Between the three locations, in this weather land.
Punta Arenas, cold and wet, a day to stay inside,
Aalborg, warm and sunny, but with clouds that won't subside.
Viña del Mar, a haven, a place to be,
Tomorrow, with its clear skies, and warm, sunny sea.

So if you had to choose, where to be tomorrow,
Viña del Mar, would be the place to borrow,
A day of sunshine, and gentle, soothing air,
A perfect day, without a single care.

Aalborg, Dinamarca, con nubes tan brillantes,

Pero Viña del Mar, con una historia diferente,

Viento máximo de 5.7, una brisa suave y reconfortante,

Las diferencias son marcadas, los contrastes tan grandiosos,
Entre las tres ubicaciones, en esta tierra del clima.
Punta Arenas, frío y húmedo, un día para quedarse adentro,
Aalborg, cálido y soleado, pero con nubes que no se disipan.
Viña del Mar, un refugio, un lugar para estar,
Mañana, con sus cielos claros, y un sol cálido y soleado.

Así que si tuvieras que elegir, dónde estar mañana,

Un día de sol, y una brisa suave y reconfortante,
