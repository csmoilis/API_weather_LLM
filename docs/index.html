Tomorrow's forecast, a tale to tell,
Of three locations, with weather to compel.

Max temp of 7.3, a chilly day.

But winds of 28.3, make it hard to clutch.
Humidity high, at 79%, a misty veil,
Aalborg, Denmark, with a warmer gale.
Max temp of 14.6, a pleasant sight,
No precipitation, a sunny delight.
Winds of 20.2, a gentle breeze,
Clouds at 50%, a partial ease.
Viña del Mar, Chile, with a warmer shore,
Max temp of 16.5, a temperature to adore.
No precipitation, a dry and sunny day,
Winds of 8.4, a gentle sway.
Clouds at 59%, a partly cloudy sky,
Humidity high, at 90%, a tropical sigh.
So where to be, tomorrow's the test,
Viña del Mar, with its warm and sunny nest.
Aalborg, a close second, with its gentle breeze,
But Punta Arenas, with its chill, brings us to our knees.
